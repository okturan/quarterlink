# Frog Feast test game

`cps1frog.zip` is a transformed build of Charles Doty's Frog Feast for Capcom CPS-1, used as a freely distributable FBNeo runtime and multiplayer transport test.

- Original: `https://frogfeast.rastersoft.net/Files/CPS1Frog.zip`
- Original SHA-256: `53706e7d86ae4f998981bfbc0f3f0058df014a820895bd7a9b09c65c7f87f14f`
- Transformed SHA-256: `d1b07899f946336768ed68ff734e1b843a1083608887c20e2456bbb0a91916bf`
- Permission: `https://frogfeast.rastersoft.net/CPS1Src.html`

The original `ff*.bin` members are renamed to the `frog*.bin` names expected by FBNeo's `cps1frog` driver; their contents are unchanged. Copyright 2006 Charles Doty.
